01 / About

Bridging brains, robots, and the people they serve.

I work at the intersection of robotics, neuroadaptive interfaces, and human–robot interaction — designing systems where humans and robots interact safely, clearly, and intuitively.

My research centers on human–robot interaction (HRI) and brain–computer interfaces (BCI), translating human intent into reliable, interpretable robot behavior. Alongside this I build the full robotics stack — perception and machine vision, motion planning and control, and learning from demonstration.

I care about robot behavior that is deterministic, observable, and trustworthy — not flashy demos, but systems people can actually rely on.
